it's all about me!

Hi there! My date of birth is 26th February 1990. I have borned and lived during 27 years in Turkey. Since december 2017 I have lived in the Netherlands and from now on I will keep living here. My home is in Hoogstraat 8, Leersum.

I am married for almost two years. My lovely wife is Nedime. We have no kids yet. She and I are very new in the Netherlands, that's why we are trying to be naturalized and to build a new and bright future for us. By the way we love to be here.

I had studied at Fatih Science and Anatolian Private High School for four years as a full scholarship student. On 2008, I ranked top 27 among over 1,5 million students in the university entrance exam in Turkey and I deserved to get into Bilkent University Law Faculty with the full scholarship. I got my bachelor diploma on 2013 with success.

After my graduation, I started to work at Turgut Ozal University as a teaching assistant. Unfortunately, the Turkish Government shut our university down on my wedding day in mid-2016. Then despite lots of political obstacles, I started to do attorney trainee. I have been making a strong impression on Ankara Bar Association and Gazi Law Firm. Although I was just an attorney trainee, the clients of the law firm have been wanted to meet me when they had a trouble. But again, the government stopped my attorney traineeship in October 2017. Soon after, I went to Greece with a heavy heart. Then I came to the Netherlands to apply for asylum.
